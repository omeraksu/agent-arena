## 1. HACKER (Neon Yesil)

**Subject:** A hyper-detailed, cinematic art portrait of an anonymous cyberpunk hacker, designed as a collectible NFT card. The central figure is a hooded character seen from a low-angle, sitting in a dark, server-lined room. Their face is obscured by shadows, but the intense, toxic green glow from multiple holographic screens reflects off their high-tech visor and cybernetic implants on their neck and hands.

**Art Style:** Photorealistic, dark cyberpunk aesthetic, Unreal Engine 5 rendering, Octane Render quality, hyper-detailed, intricate, elegant.

**Environment & Atmosphere:** The room is a messy data haven filled with exposed wires, server racks with blinking LEDs, and optic cables snaking across the floor. The air is thick with a hazy, volumetric fog, pierced by sharp god-rays of neon green light emanating from the holographic interfaces. The overall mood is intense, clandestine, and powerful.

**Key Elements & Details:**
- **Holographic Interfaces:** Instead of a simple terminal, the hacker is surrounded by three-dimensional, multi-layered holographic displays. These interfaces show cascading binary code, fluctuating stock market graphs, glitched-out security warnings (e.g., "ACCESS GRANTED," "FIREWALL BREACHED"), and complex network maps in glowing green.
- **Character Details:** The hacker's hoodie is made of a futuristic, slightly reflective fabric. Their hands, resting on a non-visible keyboard, are covered in fingerless gloves, revealing glowing circuit tattoos or cybernetic knuckles. A cascade of "Matrix-style" digital rain, rendered in 3D, should appear to fall around the character and dissolve into the holographic screens.
- **Lighting & Color:** The scene is overwhelmingly dominated by a radiant, toxic green neon glow. This should be the primary light source. Add subtle secondary light accents of electric blue and deep violet from server status lights to create contrast and depth. Anamorphic lens flares should streak horizontally from the brightest points of light.

**Composition & Framing:**
- **NFT Card Frame:** The entire scene is framed by a sleek, metallic border made of dark carbon fiber and polished obsidian. The border should have finely etched, glowing green circuit board patterns. At the bottom, a clean, bold title reads "HACKER" in a modern, sans-serif font.
- **Shot:** Cinematic low-angle shot, looking up at the hacker to give them a sense of power and authority. Shallow depth of field, with the foreground elements (like cables on the floor) slightly blurred, the hacker in sharp focus, and the background servers softly out of focus.

## 5. GLITCH (Kirmizi+Cyan/Chaos)

Hyper-detailed, ultra-realistic, 8K, cinematic shot of a collectible NFT card, embodying a chaotic glitchcore and cyberpunk aesthetic.

**Core Subject:** The central focus is a luminous, three-dimensional abstract sigil of chaos, appearing as if it's made of fractured glass and solidified digital code. It emanates a powerful, unstable energy.

**Color & Lighting:** The color palette is strictly dominated by vibrant, electric reds and luminous, piercing cyans, clashing against a deep, matte black background. Dramatic, high-contrast neon lighting with volumetric light rays and lens flare. The central sigil glows intensely from within, casting sharp, glitching shadows.

**Aesthetic & Effects (Highest Detail):**
- **Glitch Overload:** The entire scene is rich with extreme glitch effects: cascading, broken scanlines; severe chromatic aberration separating red and cyan channels; intense pixel sorting and data moshing artifacts stretching across the frame.
- **Screen Simulation:** The image is framed as if viewed through a dirty, cracked CRT monitor screen. Include subtle screen-door effect (pixel grid) and flickering holographic static in the foreground.
- **HUD Elements:** Integrated into the composition are fragmented, semi-transparent UI elements in a sharp, futuristic typographic style. These include flickering warning symbols, corrupted progress bars, and streams of unreadable binary code that seem to "bleed" pixels.
- **Texture & Depth:** Create a tangible sense of depth. Floating digital embers and sharp, out-of-focus geometric shards in the foreground. The background is a complex, dark void with faint, distorted architectural lines.

**Composition:** Dynamic and slightly off-center composition, creating a sense of unease. The central sigil is the main focal point, with glitch effects and HUD elements swirling around it, pulling the viewer's eye inward. Rule of thirds applied for a professional, cinematic feel.

**Keywords:** glitchcore, cyberpunk, datamosh, pixel sorting, cinematic, high contrast, neon glow, volumetric lighting, HUD, UI, digital artifact, chaotic, screen distortion, electric red, luminous cyan, abstract sigil, 8K, ultra-detailed, intricate, professional photography.
